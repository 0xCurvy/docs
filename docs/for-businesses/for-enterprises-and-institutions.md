## Enterprise capabilities

### White-label user domains

Launch enterprise-branded programmable identities.

Example:

`satoshi.yourcompany.io`  
instead of  
`satoshi.curvy.name`

This allows you to:

- Own the identity layer
- Control user namespaces
- Align on-chain identity with your brand
- Define programmable transaction behavior at the domain level

---

### White-label application domain

Deploy a fully re-branded application interface under your own domain.

This includes:

- Custom frontend styling
- Enterprise UX adjustments
- Integrated workflows
- Institutional-grade onboarding logic

Curvy infrastructure operates in the background while your brand remains fully visible.

---

### Custom fee models

Define pricing and settlement logic aligned with your business model.

Options include:

- Ecosystem-level fee configuration
- Subsidized privacy flows
- Revenue sharing structures

You control how value flows through your ecosystem.

---

### Custom privacy aggregator routing

Control where and how funds are aggregated inside the Privacy Aggregator.

This enables:

- Dedicated aggregation pools
- Jurisdiction-specific routing
- Institutional liquidity separation
- Controlled exit pathways

---

### Custom networks and tokens

Support only the chains and assets relevant to your organization.

- Restrict allowed networks
- Whitelist approved tokens
- Define asset-level risk policies
- Align infrastructure with internal mandates

---

### Custom compliance rules

Curvy includes a modular compliance framework that can be aligned with your institutional requirements.

Configurable components include:

- KYB / KYC integrations
- KYT integrations
- Risk scoring thresholds
- Sanctions screening
- Policy-based transaction filtering
- Retroactive compliance models
- Viewing key frameworks for full audit capability
